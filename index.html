Bounce

Inspired by the neon colors of cyberpunk artworks, Bounce is an interactive, visual presentation that is best paired with lofi music.

The large particles oscillate back and forth at a set speed determined by where you click inside the canvas. Clicking towards the top left corner sets a faster oscillation speed, while clicking towards the bottom right corner sets a slower oscillation speed.

Music: "Take me away" - WYS
Art: Cyberpunk 2077
